Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance can I make a reservation?

You can make reservations up to 30 days in advance. For special events or large groups, please call us directly to discuss availability beyond this period.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change. You can cancel your reservation free of charge up to 2 hours before your booking time. For no-shows or late cancellations, we may require a deposit for future reservations.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! Please mention any dietary restrictions or allergies in the special requests section when making your reservation, and our chefs will be happy to accommodate your needs.

Is there a time limit for tables?

During peak hours, we kindly request that tables be returned within 2 hours. However, if we don't have waiting guests, you're welcome to enjoy your time with us for longer.

Can I modify my reservation after booking?

Yes, you can modify your reservation by calling us directly at least 2 hours before your booking time. Changes are subject to availability.
