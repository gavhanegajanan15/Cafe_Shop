Our Story

Founded in 2008, Cafe House began as a small neighborhood coffee shop with a big dream: to create a community space where people could enjoy exceptional coffee and food in a warm, welcoming environment.

Today, we continue to source the finest ingredients, craft each drink with care, and bake our pastries fresh daily. We're proud to be a gathering place for friends, families, and coffee enthusiasts.
